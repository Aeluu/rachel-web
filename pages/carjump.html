Carjump (now known as Free2Move)

Carjump is a carsharing application that lets you book multiple carsharing providers on one app. This helps if you are registered with more than one carsharing app and your only goal is to get a booking quickly, because it shows you every result on one page. Carjump was successfully acquired by Citroen in 2016.

What did I do here?

I was the UI/UX designer for Carjump. I helped design the iOS and Android application, and worked on many of the features they still have today. The features that I worked on were: Onboarding, Registration, Booking flow, Check-out flow and Provider list integration.
