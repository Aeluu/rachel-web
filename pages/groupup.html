Groupup

Groupup is an application that lets you buy and sell with your coworkers and other professionals in your area. We verify each person that creates an account so our users feel safer when they have an exchange with each other. Currently Groupup is only active in San Francisco and iOS users. We went from product idea to execution in just 4 months!

What do I do here?

I'm the product designer for Groupup. I designed the iOS application and do anything design-related, such as the marketing material for the iOS page. I also developed and deployed the website, but I think it could use a little bit more design work on my end.
