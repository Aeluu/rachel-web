ParkU

ParkU is a parking application that lets you book parking spaces all over europe. It is most popular in the Netherlands, Germany and Austria. One feature that was added while I was working there was the "street parking" feature, which lets users pay for their street parking through their phone rather than fishing out cash for the meter.
